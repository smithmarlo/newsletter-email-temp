The Metropolitan Museum of Art

With more than 2 million artworks in the collections, the Metropolitan Museum of Art can be overwhelming to explore on your own. Get a personalized tour of New York City's premier museum with a private art historian guide who shows you the highlights accompanied by commentary.

The Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty Enlightening the World was a gift of friendship from the people of France to the people of the United States and is a universal symbol of freedom and democracy.The Statue of Liberty was dedicated on October 28, 1886, designated as a National Monument in 1924 and restored for her centennial on July 4, 1986.

Radio City Music Hall

Secure an advance ticket for this behind-the-scenes tour of Manhattan’s legendary Radio City Music Hall. Save time and guarantee a place for your preferred time by prebooking, and gain insight into this historical entertainment hall that you wouldn’t get by attending a show.


Broadway Theaters and Times Square

Don’t settle for any average Broadway tour while you're visiting New York City. This Broadway theaters and Times Square tour gives you an insider’s look with a guide who is actually a Broadway performer. Learn about the history and happenings of New York City’s famous theater district, and hear the backstage stories about the stars of yesterday and today.
